SEGMENTATION DATASET

The main purpose of this dataset is to solve the semantic segmentation problem. This dataset is presented in the form of source images and binary masks. The size of images in the dataset is 1000x666 pixels. The binary mask mapping is performed automatically using the alpha masks. This is an earlier dataset where the original alpha channels are not saved. The photographs were taken in 2016.

All images have been anonymized using the copyrighted software. Anonymization of all photographs is performed using the model who has given his consent to it.

The images are represented by several regions of the country. They are made with various photo camera models, in various premises and shooting conditions, during various seasons. Most of the photographs are taken on light gray, blue or white backgrounds. The photographs are taken using professional lighting devices (flash umbrellas) or a built-in flash. The models on the photos posturize as follows:

  • Upright position
  • Sitting on a chair (different variations)
  • On the floor (different variations)
  • Taking various accessories (weapons, swords, balls, etc.)
  • The source photos have several types of portrait cropping: full length, waist-high, breast-deep, head and shoulders.
  • It is guaranteed that all photographs are unique!

MATTING DATASET

This dataset contains the high quality images in .png format. Data is represented by 4-channel images (RGB + alpha). The images are provided in high resolution (on an average, 4500 pixels along the length). Each photograph has been cut away by the professional retouch artists that guarantees an excellent quality of the image alpha channels. Even the smallest details, such as the ends of hair and the clothes laces are retained! Just as in the segmentation dataset, the photographs have been taken in different parts of the country at different time and with different types of shooting, postures, and cropping modes. This is a newer dataset, the photographs in which were taken in 2018.
